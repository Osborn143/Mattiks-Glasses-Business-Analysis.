# Mattiks Glasses Business Intelligence Dashboard
## Project Overview
This project analyzes the business performance of **Mattiks Glasses**, an e-commerce eyewear retailer.  
The objective was to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.

The analysis focuses on revenue trends, product performance, and marketing effectiveness using an interactive **Power BI dashboard**.

## Business Objectives
The dashboard was designed to answer key business questions:

- How is revenue performing over time?
- Which products and categories drive the most sales?
- Which marketing channels generate the highest returns?
- What trends can executives monitor to guide business strategy?

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of overall business performance.

Key Metrics:
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders

Visualizations:
- Revenue Trend Analysis
- Revenue by Channel
- Top Products by Revenue
- Dynamic Executive Insight Panel


### Sales & Product Performance
Focuses on understanding product-level contribution to revenue.

Key Insights:
- Revenue distribution across product categories
- Top performing products
- Profit vs Revenue comparison
- Monthly revenue trends by category

Visualizations:
- Category Revenue Breakdown
- Revenue Trend by Category
- Top Products by Revenue
- Profit vs Revenue Chart
- Product Performance Table


### Marketing Performance
Analyzes the effectiveness of marketing channels.

Key Metrics:
- Total Revenue by Channel
- Marketing Spend
- Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)

Visualizations:
- Revenue by Marketing Channel
- Channel Conversion Rate
- Campaign Performance Table
- Marketing Insight Narrative


## Data Model
The dataset follows a **star schema design** to optimize performance and scalability.

Tables include:
- Fact_Sales
- Dim_Customers
- Dim_Product
- Dim_Channel
- Dim_Date

This structure allows efficient calculation of KPIs and time intelligence metrics.


## Key DAX Measures
Some of the core measures used in the analysis include:

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Revenue Year-over-Year Growth
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)

Dynamic insight measures were also implemented to automatically generate executive summaries.
